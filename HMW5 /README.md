6. I compared Amazon and Alibaba to see how they apply the four basic design principles:
Repetition: Both platforms use repetition through their signature color palettes (Amazon's orange/dark blue and Alibaba's orange/white). The search bar and navigation menus stay in the same place on every page, which makes it feel consistent.
Contrast: They use dark text on white backgrounds to make sure product details are easy to read. Important buttons like "Buy Now" or "Add to Cart" usually have bright colors that pop against the background.
Proximity: I noticed that product images, prices, and star ratings are grouped tightly together. This makes it obvious which information belongs to which product without needing extra lines or borders.
Alignment: Everything is very structured. They use a grid system so that product cards and category lists align perfectly. This keeps the page looking organized even though there is a massive amount of data
